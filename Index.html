115 Year Heritage
Unity Nationa Bank, has a 115-year tradition of community-based service and commitment to our customers. From our simple beginnings as a small community bank in Jasper, Arkansas in 1903, we’ve grown to be recognized as the top performing bank in the nation 12 times over the past eight years as determined by leading industry publications* based on our financial performance.

We've always been the kind of bank where people like to do business and where people like to work, with a strong commitment to the communities we serve while cultivating relationships that grow and thrive together. And now, as Unity Nationa Bank, we’re doubling down on our community bank heritage while leveraging banking technology to deliver exceptional customer service.
